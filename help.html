FAQ

  • What is Pass-It-Back?

    Pass-It-Back is a real-time mapping service that is a work in progress for students who wish to provide their peers feedback for crowding spaces and approximated wait-times on campus.

  • What motivated us to make Pass-It-Back?

    With the large amount of students Texas A&M has, resturants and cafes are often extremely crowded. We wanted to give students the ability to know just how crowded a place is before they arrive.

  • Who's our target audience?

    Anyone! We expect students are likely to use it the most but anyone on campus could benefit from Pass-It-Back.

  • Can Pass-It-Back be "de-TAMUed" and transferred to any applicable spatial situation?

    Most likely, but it will need further code reviewing to ensure that it will be compatible regardless of certain parameters specific to Pass-It-Back

  • Why not just use a Google Form?

    The collector app is the main draw of this as it records your geolocation. The "crowding" portion is simply a relevant example of how stationary poionts may be mapped.
